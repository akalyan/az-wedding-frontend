Accommodations

Accommodation in Seattle books up very far in advance for Labor Day weekend, so please find your accommodation soon!

We have a package deal at the Embassy Suites in Bellevue. You can make your reservation here. Rooms are from $159 to $179 per night. If you stay here you should plan on renting a car.

Alternatively, there are great short-term apartment rentals in Seattle. You can even stay in our neighborhood, Capitol Hill. Other neighborhoods we recommend are Belltown, Queen Anne, & South Lake Union. If you stay in Capitol Hill you will be near us, in walking distance from restaurants, supermarkets, and downtown, and you can walk to the ceremony and take a cab to the reception. You wouldn't need to rent a car if you don't want to if you stay in Seattle.

Please don't hesitate to reach out to us if you have any questions - celebrateatoz [at] gmail [dot] com.
